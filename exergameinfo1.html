Exergame Information

This exergame is the Flappy Bird Game.

How it works:
Your movements control the movements of the Flappy Bird! Move up to move the bird up, and down to move the bird down. Remember: Don't let the bird touch the pipes! Earn one point every time the bird goes past the pipes. However, once the Flappy Bird touches the pipes... it's "Game Over" for you!

What you need:
A competitive streak for winning! Last as long as you can and accumulate as many points as possible. Remember to have a cup of water beside you in case you want to have another go!
