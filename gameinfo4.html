Exercise Information

This exercise is the lunge right exercise.

What you need to do:
Follow the video feed for instructions on what to do! You can press the "mute" button to mute the audio instructions if they are not necessary. All you have to do is turn to your left, put your right leg straight out behind your left and bend your left knee to lunge!

Number of times exercise should be carried out:
10 times

Difficulty level:
Medium! This exercise requires good balance, as well as motor skills.

What you need:
Nothing! All you need is a positive mindset, a cup of water by your side, and the enthusiasm to carry out this exercise!
