With the increasing demand for paediatric physiotherapy in Singapore and the lack of such specialized services, Little Stretch aims to provide accessible and affordable physiotherapy for all children. Little Stretch is committed to providing quality online paediatric physiotherapy to allow your little ones to receive timely physiotherapy exercises to stretch their body and improve its function. We specialize in interactive and quality exercises, that: • Address functional impairments, including muscle tone, strength, coordination, balance and posture, mobility and achieving an optimal range of motion • Provides user-friendly guides to hands-on techniques on exercises • Tracks movement and provides feedback in real time • Personalized exercises • Makes receiving physiotherapy fun and easy • Make seeking appropriate physiotherapy for your little ones convenient and worry-free

Relief is closer than you think No one should have to live a life of pain. And that's what Little Stretch is all about--making sure that your child lives a full life without any aches and pains bothering them. That may seem hard to achieve, but with Little Stretch's tailored website, we have just what your child needs. Unlike in-person appointments, which are scheduled every now and then, the web-based method of physiotherapy allows for more consistent efforts. Once every day for 30 minutes is all it takes to see some visible progress in 4-6 weeks.
